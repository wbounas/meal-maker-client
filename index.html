Meal[Maker] v. 1

Meal[Maker] allows you to easily keep track of all of the ingredients inside of your fridge.

NEW FEATURES COMING SOON

Version 2: Getting hungry throughout the day? Plan dinner ahead of time, and create meals based off of the ingredients you know you have in your fridge!

Version 3: Are you planning on cooking that special meal, but you know you need to grab chickpeas and pita from the grocery store? Now, create meals with ingredients that you don't have in your fridge, and they'll automatically be added to a shopping list for quick and easy access!
